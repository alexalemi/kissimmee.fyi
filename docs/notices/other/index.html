The applicant’s responsible official and mailing address are: Tyler Hitchcck, General Manager, Xpanda Foam, LLC, 7557 Currency Drive, Orlando, Florida 32809. Facility Location: The applicant operates the existing Orlando Facility, which is located in Orange County at 1090 Gills Drive in Orlando, Florida. Project: The applicant applied on June 4, 2026, to the EPD for an air construction permit modification and an initial Title V air operation permit. The existing expanded polystyrene (EPS) foam block and board manufacturing facility consists of a pressurized steam expander, shape molding presses, a block-molding machine, storage bins, and hot-wire cutting units used in the manufacturing process. Pollutants emitted by the manufacturing activities are volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Manufacturing operations at the facility are currently conducted in accordance with Air Operation Permit No. 0951404-002-AO. The air construction permit authorizes a modification of Emissions Unit (EU) 001 – Insulated Board and Block Manufacturing, to authorize increased production hours of operation in order to meet increased product demand. The increase in manufacturing hours will increase annual emissions of volatile organic compounds VOCs and make the facility a Title V source of VOC emissions. This initial Title V air operation permit will provide the initial Title V permit for the facility. Permitting Authority: Applications for air construction permits are subject to review in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 403, Florida Statutes (F.S.) and Chapters 62-4, 62-210 and 62-212 of the Florida Administrative Code (F.A.C.). The proposed project is not exempt from air permitting requirements and an air permit is required to perform the proposed work. Applications for Title V air operation permits are subject to review in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 403, Florida Statutes (F.S.) and Chapters 62-4, 62-210 and 62-213, of the Florida Administrative Code (F.A.C.). The proposed project is not exempt from air permitting requirements and a Title V air operation permit is required to operate the facility. The Orange County Environmental Protection Division (EPD) is the Permitting Authority responsible for making a permit determination for this project. The Permitting Authority’s physical and mailing address is: 3165 McCrory Place, Suite 200, Orlando, Florida 32803. The Permitting Authority’s telephone number is (407) 836-1400 and the email address is AirPermitsOrangeCounty@ocfl.net.
