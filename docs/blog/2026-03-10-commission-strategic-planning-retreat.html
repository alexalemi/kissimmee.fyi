Kissimmee City Commission Strategic Planning Retreat Continuation - 2026-03-10 Recording

Published: March 18, 2026 | kissimmee.fyi

The Kissimmee City Commission held a continuation of their Annual Strategic Planning Retreat on March 10, 2026. This recording was not available on the CivicClerk Public Meeting Portal. It was obtained via public records request.

The retreat is split into two parts:

  • Part 1 (MP3, ~2 hours)
  • Part 2 (M4A, ~23 minutes)

Topics Discussed

The retreat focused on governance and internal commission operations. Key topics included:

  • Action Agenda with Deadlines — Review of the 18-month strategic priorities
    action plan developed with department heads, with milestones for this fiscal
    year and next.
  • Mid-Year Strategic Planning Workshop — Proposal to hold a follow-up
    workshop in June, before the budget workshop, to check progress on priorities.
  • Meeting Procedures — Discussion of parliamentary procedures, the role of
    motions, and the order of public comment vs. commissioner discussion.
  • House Rules and Civility — Extended discussion on adopting a code of
    conduct and civility for commission interactions, both during and outside of
    meetings.
  • Public Comment Procedures — Discussion about whether speakers should
    address the commission as a body rather than targeting individual
    commissioners.
  • Social Media — Counsel on the risks of commissioner engagement with social
    media and the importance of controlling messaging.
  • Crisis Management — Discussion of security concerns and the reality of
    threats faced by elected officials.
