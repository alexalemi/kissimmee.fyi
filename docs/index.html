Community information and resources for Kissimmee, Florida
Disclaimer: This is not an official City of Kissimmee website. The information provided here is collected from public sources and provided as a community service. For official information, please visit the City of Kissimmee website.

Public Notices

  • Planning Advisory Board Notices
    Public notices for Planning Advisory Board meetings, including zoning changes, land use amendments, variances, and development proposals. The PAB reviews applications and makes recommendations to the City Commission.
  • City Commission Notices
    Public notices for City Commission meetings and public hearings. The City Commission is the main legislative body that makes final decisions on zoning, land use, and other city matters.
  • Osceola County BCC Notices
    Public notices for Osceola County Board of County Commissioners meetings. County-level decisions that may affect Kissimmee residents.
  • Other Boards & Committees
    Public notices for other city and county boards, committees, and commissions that don't have dedicated pages yet. Includes various advisory boards, special districts, and public hearing notices.
  • Miscellaneous Notices
    All other public notices related to Kissimmee, including legal notices, public announcements, and general government publications.

All notices are automatically updated every 6 hours from the Florida Public Notices database.

Civic Education

  • Blog
    Articles explaining planning and zoning processes, development trends, and how to participate in local government decisions.

This site aggregates public information to make it more accessible to Kissimmee residents. Data is automatically collected and updated regularly.
